About Us

Agility Solutions is a Virginia based company started by software developer Siva Gopalan, who has over a decade of experience in designing and developing software.

Siva has worked mostly on enterprise web development projects and has primarily used Java/J2EE, jQuery, Android, Business Process Management (BPM), WebServices and host of other technologies to implement applications. He has worked on large scale enterprise web applications in the recent years and more recently has been focusing on mobile web projects.Since June 2015, Abhay Bakshi has partnered with Agility Solutions with his execution group in Kharadi, Pune Metropolitan Area.  

Siva has a Sun Certified Enterprise Architect and Sun Certified Java Programmer Certification and is an alumnus of the Johns Hopkins Carey Business School where he earned his Masters in Information Systems.

Abhay has pursued his advanced degree M.S. Computer Engineering in 1995-1996; and has lived in the United States for 15 years until 2010 serving healthcare, finance and education domains in various capacities.

Abhay is a Certified Scaled Agile (SAFe) Agilist and has worked with Johns Hopkins University (Chief Scientist: Marty Hall), George Mason University and Florida Atlantic University. He has also developed two full-scale (~700 pages) drafts of "OOP in C++" (2004) with the esteemed McGraw Hill Education Group. Agility Solutions mission is to create value for individuals, businesses and organizations by providing software services,design,architecture and consultancy expertise.
